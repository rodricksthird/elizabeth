About Elizabeth!

Hello friend! I am glad you are here! I hope you will enjoy looking at my site as much I enjoyed building it! I have been a front-end web developer for two years now, and let me tell you, the journey to get here was not easy! After a multitude of unfulfilling jobs unrelated to tech (neither of my degrees were in Computer Science) over the course of 10 years, I decided to make a career change. I completed 2 different full-stack coding bootcamps, and now I have a job that I absolutely love and adore! I work as a front-end developer, and am hoping to become a full-stack engineer by 2021. I am also a photographer- I mainly shoot cityscapes and architecture. You can visit my Instagram to see all of my pictures, or view some I am selling on iStock by Getty. Please visit my portfolio link to view my coding work. Enjoy!
